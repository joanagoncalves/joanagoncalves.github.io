Travel tips

Walking / Cycling

Walking to the EEMCS faculty from the Delft station takes 25-30min, cycling takes about 7min.

Public transport

Buses 40, 69, 174 from the Delft station will leave you at the door of the EEMCS faculty building 36 (stop Mekelpark). Cross the street and walk straight ahead through the opening in the Civil Engineering building until you find the Computer Science and Math building 28.
