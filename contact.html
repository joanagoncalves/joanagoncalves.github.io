Travel tips

Cycling / driving

Cycling and driving are not allowed on the Mekelweg. Take the Leeghwaterstraat or the Rotterdamseweg instead.

Public transport

Buses 40, 69, 174 from the Delft station will leave you at the door (sept out at stop Mekelpark).
